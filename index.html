A note from us

Three years ago,
we built something small.

No roadmap. No investors. Just a hunch that people needed more breathing room in their day. 3,500 of you found us anyway. 26,000 newsletter reads. More podcast listeners than we ever expected.

Then Android launched "Pause Point." Native. Built into the hardware. Already on your phone. We're not going to pretend that didn't happen.

But here's the thing. PausePoint was never just an app. It was a container for something bigger we were building toward. The writing, the conversations, the data, the stories you shared with us. That was always the real work. That work isn't stopping. It's just moving somewhere it can breathe.

The newsletter continues. The podcast continues. Same voice, new home. Join the waitlist and you'll be the first to know where we're headed, plus something we've been sitting on that we think you'll love.
